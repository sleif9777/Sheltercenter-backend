Dog Availability

  • Dogs are first-come, first-served. Appointments are not dog-specific, and availability is never guaranteed.
  • Our website updates automatically; dogs removed are likely adopted.
  • More dogs are available in person than listed online. Flexibility increases your chance of a successful adoption.

Directions

  • Address: 13400 Old Creedmoor Rd, Wake Forest, NC (behind Harris Teeter, NC-98 & Old Creedmoor).
  • Entry: Enter through the far back left gate and check in with the greeter.
  • Respect neighbors: Do not use the closed wooden gate or neighbors' driveways.

Dress & Comfort

  • Dress for outdoor appointments according to the weather; dogs may jump in excitement.
  • Bring water to stay hydrated, especially on hot days.

Restrooms & Supplies

  • Restrooms: A port-a-potty is on-site, and public restrooms are available at Harris Teeter.
  • Supplies: Bring a collar and leash.
  • Kibble: Our dogs are fed from a variety of donated kibble. We recommend a protein-focused, grain-inclusive kibble at home.
  • Saving Grace Supply Co.: Located at NC-98 & Six Forks Rd for essential items.

Adoption Fee

  • Fee: $395 (cash or credit/debit), plus any additional donation listed on their bio. Exact cash preferred; extra donations are welcome.
  • Includes: Spay/neuter, microchip, vaccinations, preventatives, and baseline wellness exam.

Training & Adjustment

  • Crate experience: Many dogs have crate training, but housetraining is not guaranteed.
  • Transition period: Expect your dog to need time to adjust to your routine.
  • Rule of 3s: Use the Rule of 3s for introducing a dog to your home and family. See our guide to the Rule of 3s for more information.

Bringing Your Dog

  • On-leash only: All introductions occur on-leash in the parking lot.
  • Car or parking lot waiting: If bringing your dog, ensure the weather is cool (Nov-Mar) for car waiting; otherwise, dogs must wait in the parking lot with a family member.
  • Notify us: Let us know in advance if you plan to bring a dog.

Renters

  • Breed restrictions: Standard breed restrictions apply unless your landlord provides a written exception.
  • Responsibility: Verify any restrictions on size, weight, age, breed, or number of pets before your visit.
