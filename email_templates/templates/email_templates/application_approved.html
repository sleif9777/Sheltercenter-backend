Scheduling Your Appointment

  • Appointments may be scheduled up to two weeks in advance.
  • Approved applications remain on file for one year (your application will expire on {{ approved_until_display }}).
  • Schedule online at www.savinggracencscheduler.com.
  • You will receive a one-time passcode by email (check Spam/Junk if it does not arrive within two minutes).
  • Email is our primary method of communication; phone support is not available.

If you experience technical issues, email adoptions@savinggracenc.org with specific details. Screenshots are especially helpful.

Dog Availability & Selection

  • Applications are general interest, not for a specific dog.
  • Dogs are adopted on a first-come, first-served basis.
  • We do not hold dogs for appointments or future dates.
  • We do not recommend getting attached to a specific dog ahead of your appointment.
  • Appointments last about one hour and include meeting multiple dogs.
  • Any dog listed on our website is currently available.
  • Dogs removed from the website are no longer available.

We are unable to track wishlists or confirm future availability by email. Flexibility is encouraged, as we often have more dogs available than are listed online.

Puppies & “Fun Size” Dogs

Puppies

  • Puppies typically arrive on Wednesdays.
  • Once they arrive, they are available every day until adopted.
  • We do not hold puppies for future appointments.
  • For the best chance of meeting a specific puppy, schedule the earliest available appointment on their arrival date.
  • Any exceptions will be clearly noted in the puppy’s bio.
  • Adult size cannot be guaranteed.

Fun Size Dogs

  • Dogs labeled “Fun Size” (most, but not all under 20 lbs, as noted in the bio) are housed in off-site foster care.
  • Fun Size dogs are available for adoption appointments Fridays and Saturdays only.

Important Policies

  • Additional donations listed in a dog's bio are mandatory.
  • We don't offer trial adoptions. Our Sleepover program is not designed or intended for this.
  • We do not transport or ship dogs.
  • Adoptions must be completed in person by the approved applicant.
  • Friends or family members should not adopt on someone else's behalf.
  • We strongly encourage young children to attend appointments to ensure a kid-friendly fit.
  • Please let us know in advance if you need mobility or accessibility accommodations.

Location & Hours

  • All adoption appointments are held on-site at 13400 Old Creedmoor Rd, Wake Forest, NC.
  • Hours:
    • Mon, Tue, Wed, Fri: 12-6pm
    • Thu: 1-6pm
    • Sat: 12-3pm

Adoption Fees & Outcomes

  • Adoption fee: $395, plus any additional donation listed on bio
  • Payment accepted: cash or credit/debit only
  • Valid ID required
  • Adoptable: Dog goes home the same day
  • Foster-to-Adopt: Heartworm-positive dogs, you foster until treatment is complete
  • Chosen/Reserved: Dog requires additional veterinary care before adoption or foster-to-adopt

Partnerships

We are proud to partner with Boehringer-Ingelheim (BI), who offers a free preventative care program for adopted dogs. BI will contact you after adoption with more information about their services, and you will have the option to opt out of future communications.

Additionally, we use a private scheduling program owned and created by one of our volunteers. Your application data will be stored securely in the database and used solely for scheduling purposes.
